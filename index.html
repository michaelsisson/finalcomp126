CSI Video & Security is a family-owned business with over 17 years of experience placing CCTV cameras in all sectors. We are expert providers of full service installations, video surveillance systems for monitoring and analytics, access control, intercom, and audio systems servicing locations in Broward, Miami, and West Palm Beach.

Video security and video analytics enables you to know what is happening in real time through technology. Our vision is to make your environment safer and your security measures more effective by giving you visibility to security events as they occur.
